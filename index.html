About Us

BlueKing Management is composed of a team of seasoned professionals, including former senior merchant fleet officers, marine technicians, quality auditors, commercial operators, industry specialists, ship owners, and ship managers, all dedicated to implementing the best management practices.

We excel in providing ship management services in a cost-effective manner while prioritizing a strong safety culture and environmental stewardship. Our independence allows us the flexibility to deliver customized solutions. Our goal is to be a reliable partner and grow in collaboration with our clients.

Our processes are designed to be straightforward and efficient, ensuring that we produce results rather than excuses. Grounded in our core values and best seamanship practices, we continually refine our strategies to adapt to the ever-changing maritime industry, driven by our Vision of Trusted Navigation and Trusted Shipping and our Purpose of Creating Value.
